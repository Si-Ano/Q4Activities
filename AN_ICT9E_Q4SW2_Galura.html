Some Basic Farenheit to Celsius Conversions


Farenheit (ºF) Celsius (ºC) Description

Fun Facts!


  • People commonly use Celsius as their unit of measure for temperature
  • The coldest temperature ever recorded was in Antarctica. It was -89.2ºC or -32ºF
  • Zero Kelvin or Abosolute Zero does not posses heat energy, thus being the coldest theoretical temperature
